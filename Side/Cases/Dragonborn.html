About

The Dragonborn is a profecy based entity who killed a dragon that was terrifying people of Tamriel, he was most present in 4E 200 when the dragon menace was high, he was and still is an unpredictable foe having the elements, his weapons and an ancient power known as the thu'um or shouts these powers have multiple stages of power and he seems to get ever more powerful as he grows, keep great caution when nearing this entity
